Welcome to Say It Loud Club Community Organisation. Formed in 1994 by Aloysius Ssali a Ugandan gay activist, this organisation has led important campaigns against homophobia particularly in Uganda and Africa in general. We have been serving the LGBT communities for over a 19 years. In Uganda, Our organization led the first campaigns against homophobia in Colleges and in Business organisations due to lack of diversity promotions
