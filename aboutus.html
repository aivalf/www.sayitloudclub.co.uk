Say It Loud Club seeks protect and promote the rights of LGBT in Uganda and Africa in general. Reaching out to those seeking asylum in the UK based on their sexualities and trying to help those persecuted in their own countries simply because they were born gay.

aims and objectives of SILC understandging, awareness, protection, support and implemantation of the rights of gay people in Uganda and worldwide. This involves speaking out against homophobia and general injustices against gay people, research, advice, Publicity, lobbying and open staging open campagns and enforcements of gay values worldwide covering the following issues:

  • Sexism and gender inequality
  • Homophobia and transphobia
  • Prejudice and discriminations
  • Asylum and Refugees immigration and settlements
  • Counselling to victims of sexual abuse
  • Counselling to victims of torture
